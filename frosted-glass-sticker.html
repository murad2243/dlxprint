Frosted Glass Stickers

About

Frosted Stickers Dubai, Sharjah

Frosted sticker or frosted office glass and glass partition sticker film gives the appearance of frosted or opaque glass. It provides privacy and security and does not compromise light or views.

Frosted glass sticker is easy to clean, with regular glass cleaning products.
Frosted glass sticker film is long lasting, withstanding sunlight and UV damage.

Frosted vinyl stickers are generally used for enhancing privacy of boardrooms, obscuring view into personal areas by application of the film on any partition or glass surface. It also gives a face lift to your old office see-through glass at a minimal cost compared to blinds or curtains.

Reverse Cut Frosted Sticker

Specifications

Your design is cut out from the frosted vinyl material such that one can directly see through from anywhere the image or graphics were placed.

The standard shape of this decal will be square or rectangle with the artwork elements cut from the bigger shape.

The sticker will be pre-spaced and masked with a transfer tape before installation.

Cutout Style

Standard Cut

The shape of your decal will be a standard square/rectangle with your design elements cut out from the bigger shape.

Standard Cut Frosted Sticker

Specifications

The letters or logo will be cut out to exact shape from the frosted vinyl. The inside elements from the artwork will be removed out to avoid filling and making it solid.

As can be seen from the image, the inside of the ‘p’ is cut out instead of leaving it solid.

It shall bear no color and will just have the frosted translucent effect.

Cutout Style

Exact Cut

The graphics and letters on your decal will be cut to exact shape with inner spaces weeded/picked out.

Printed Frosted Sticker

Specifications

The artwork is directly printed onto the frosted vinyl material surface using four-color printing process.

This allows to maintain all colors used in the design at the same time allowing some light through the printed and unprinted part due to its translucent nature.

Cutout Style

Square/Rectangle

The shape of your decal will be a standard square/rectangle cut to any custom size.

Custom

The shape of your decal will be custom to match the shape of your design.

Custom with Border

The shape of your decal will be custom with a small border around the edge of your design.

Gradient Frosted Sticker

Specifications

A special kind of white ink UV printing one on a clear vinyl media giving it a gradient effect.

This is now an in-trend concept which is different from the conventional style. Makes a difference to the office meeting rooms by adding a zing to it.

Blank Frosted Sticker

Specifications

This will be an unprinted and non-cut out frosted vinyl giving it a true solid frosted effect.

Installed in its natural form without any alternations.
