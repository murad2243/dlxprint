Boat Lettering

Description

This is primarily used for numbering and lettering on boats. Could be for displaying owner or company name, working hours, registration number.

In this method, each letter or element is cut out from a solid vinyl material with no print on it.

Cutout Style

Exact Cut

The graphics and letters on your decal will be cut to exact shape with inner spaces weeded/picked out.

Boat Decals

Description

This method of branding in generally used to print intricate images on to a special quality adhesive vinyl meant for boats. The graphics are printed on one single piece of sticker then cut to exact shape with or without a thin border around it. Else it could be a standard shape like a square or rectangle.

The decal is then directly pasted as a single piece with all images and letters pre-spaced. Mainly used for displaying logos, colors, images and gradients.

Cutout Style

Square/Rectangle

The shape of your decal will be a standard square/rectangle cut to any custom size.

Custom

The shape of your decal will be custom to match the shape of your design.

Custom with Border

The shape of your decal will be custom with a small border around the edge of your design.
