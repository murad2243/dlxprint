Acrylic Frames

About

Acrylics are an excellent photo mounting medium, especially when used in conjunction with good face-mount procedures, in which the photo is mounted beneath acrylic.

Acrylic's clarity and gleam enhance your image's colors and vibrancy while also providing protection and rigidity. The finished product is stunning, long-lasting and durable, making it ideal for galleries and public display.

These stunning acrylic floating frames with beautiful metal hardware will elevate your framing game. For a borderless presentation, your artwork is sandwiched between two panels of floating acrylic. Frameless frames allow your artwork and photographs to stand out.
