Pillow & Cushion Covers

About

Cushion covers give a personal touch in decorating your living or working space. Be it your home or office couch, our durable and washable custom cushion breaks the monotony.

Personalize the cushion with your favorite memories or brand your company logo or colors to make it blend with your interior design.

Printing can be done on one side or both sides with different content or images and is completed with a concealed zip finish.

Be sure not to go unnoticed, it is indeed a delight sprucing up rooms as wedding keepsakes or gifts for loved ones.
