  • Flexible Glue Gun Wax sticks

    Flexible Glue gun wax won't break or snap which makes it ideal for envelopes and other carousel-items sent in the mail.

    The usage of these seals is infinite; can be used for any occasion - personalized gifts, gift wrapping, cards, crafts, and packaging

  • Wax Seal for envelopes

    Our Traditional Sealing Wax is easy to use. All you need is your Wax Seal Stamp and a windproof fire lighter. Simply light up the wax wick stick and allow the wax to melt and drip directly onto the flap of the envelope. Put the seal stamp on top of the melted wax, wait for 30 sec and remove the seal
