  • Full Wrap vehicle Graphics or vehicle branding, Dubai, Abu Dhabi

    A full vehicle wrap covers the entire body of the vehicle with a printed vinyl sticker. The vinyl sticker is designed in such a way so as to fit perfectly around the edges and curves of the vehicle. The vinyl sticker is printed on high resolution using special digital machines. After printing, the sticker is laminated. This is done to protect the colours from fading. The stickers are removable and do not damage the paint of body of the vehicle.
