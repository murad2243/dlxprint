Fabric Wrap

About

Traditional wrapping cloths, with exquisite fabric and contemporary design, know as Furoshiki, best used to wrap goods or gift multiple times.

Comes with hemmed edges or flash cut printed on fabric base made of cotton or polyster. They are typically square and come in a variety of sizes.

Amplify the beauty of your gift with our re-usable cloth custom printed in the design of your choice, whilst providing clints with a unique, gift-wrapping solution.
