Armband

About

Brand logos, marketing messages, slogans, and exclusive event-based designs can all be displayed on personalized armbands. Most of our customers order custom armbands for special occasions such as trade shows and exhibitions. Using promotional products at these events not only helps them attract attention from the crowd, but it also ensures that their brand is visible long after the event is over.

If your company is looking for a way to show visible support, this arm band is a great option! This elastic and polyester product is great for school spirit functions, sporting events, awareness programs, and so much more. For maximum corporate visibility on a desirable handout, personalize it with a full-color imprint of your company name, logo, brand message, and more.

    How to measure

  • Allow your arm to relax and measure (you may need assistance) around your bicep. choose a size that is just smaller than your measurement to ensure a snug fit. the elastic will expand with arm movement.
  • For example, if your arm circumference is 30cm, choose the Small size armband.
  • Remember to leave enough room for fitting over a shirt or heavy jacket.
