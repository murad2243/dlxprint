Hologram Sticker

About

Hologram stickers have a background that is a colourful and brilliant rainbow design. They often include all of the rainbow's colours, including red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. The stickers come in a range of shapes and sizes and may be used to decorate notebooks, laptops, water bottles, and other items. They are popular among children, teenagers, and young people who enjoy bright colours and fun patterns.
