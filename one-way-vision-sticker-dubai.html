  • What is one way vision sticker?

    A one way vision window decal is an adhesive vinyl material onto which graphic, text or images can be printed directly. It is also called a perforated decal which allows you to see outside the window when in store but not inside when approaching the store.

    Made possible by a feature called micro punctures wherein 50% of the vinyl material is punctured with tiny holes. This feature helps the one way vision decal to retain a vivid print image on one side yet be see-through on the other side.
